Description

Yulia Olkhovaia recreates the image of the Ancient God Dionysus, copying the 17th century painting by Peter Paul Rubens "Two Satyrs". In this manner, Yulia tries to keep the traditions and technologies of the old masters against the darkness of oblivion and denial in the 21st century. Through copying a 17th century masterpiece, Yulia comprehends not only old techniques, but also the magical nature of art.

Yulia uses only the best quality base and paints from the best manufacturers, the same ones that were used by the masters in the 17th century. This gives a guarantee that, if save correctly, the piece of art will retain its qualities even after 500 years.
